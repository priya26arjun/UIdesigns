Dream Clothing.

This app is for the users who want to customize their clothing online based on their choice.

Project Duration

20 days

My Role

Solo UX/UI Designer

Tools

Figma, Procreate

Project Overview

Dream Clothing aims to provide users a platform where they have the freedom to customize their clothing as per their choice, a platform that makes it easier for users to express themselves in form of their clothing with the choice of their prints, designs, colors and patterns.

The Problem

There are many eCommerce shopping platforms that provide ready made clothing with new designs and trends but there is a lack of platforms which provide an option where users have the freedom to select the design, print and fabric that they like. This is specially applicable for people who are either too tall or short and/or suffering obesity. These kind of people face a lot of challenge in shopping for clothing that caters to their taste and size.

The Goal

The platform solves the problems identified in ‘The Problems’ section and helps everyone and specially people with specific size requirements to be able to design clothes for themselves.

Design Process

The Design process for the Dream clothing platform can be explained by below lifecycle of the workflow.

Empathize

After meeting with many people who find it difficult to get the clothing of their choice due to availability, size issues and price sensitivity, I realized that a solution is needed.

Define

I realized that if a platform can be created where users can create their own clothing to suit their needs.

Ideate

The idea of Dream Clothing came when I was browsing the site Symplico, which provided print on-demand on selected fabrics.

Prototype

I created initial prototypes for Dream Clothing where user can choose print, then fabric and pattern as per their need and sizes.

Understand

I shared the prototypes with a set of users to receive their feedback and then I refined my prototypes accordingly.

User Research

People who want to create their own style in fashion and customize their clothing according to their taste. Also people who have difficulty finding the right clothing as per their fit online or even in specialty stores will be the right users for this platform.
